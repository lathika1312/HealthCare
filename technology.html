Technology

Technology that transforms your revenue cycle

ARC.IN platform


Achieve significant improvements in your revenue cycle through our advanced workflow engine, knowledge management system, and productivity enhancement tools. We empower you to engage effectively and leverage business intelligence, providing clear insights into your performance metrics for informed decision-making.

Optimize Operations with Our Dashboard


Our dashboard effectively manages operations by visually displaying key process health indicators. Built on a robust engine, it integrates essential metrics like productivity, attendance, TAT, quality control %, and accuracy. With a simple click, you can drill down into detailed performance data for deeper insights, enhancing decision-making. This allows our delivery leaders to focus on strategy and optimizing your revenue cycle, rather than spending time on reporting and analysis.


Revenue Cycle Services Workflow and Client Portal


Our workflow management system streamlines operations in our revenue cycle processing center and enhances collaboration with real-time reporting through a web-based portal. Enjoy unmatched transparency with a dedicated team that works seamlessly alongside yours to resolve escalations and issues, resulting in faster collections and improved revenue flow.


Knowledge Management


The arc.in Knowledge Management module offers categorized content, powerful search capabilities, and easy content updates for business rules. Clients find significant value in this tool and often implement it for their onsite operations.


People Management


The arc.in People Management module fosters a high-performance culture through collaboration, sharing, learning, and communication tools. This leads to a focused workforce and improved productivity.


RCM Business Intelligence


We channel extensive revenue cycle data into our intelligent dashboard, fostering a shared understanding of KPIs, identifying focus areas, and creating a roadmap for revenue cycle transformation.


Integrated Technology


To support high-volume transaction processing, we provide various productivity enhancement tools, including Optical Character Recognition, Intelligent Character Recognition, coding performance improvement tools, and web scraping software.

Automation to eliminate repetitive tasks
