Our purpose and Values

Our purpose


Our Purpose is to “Be the most respected healthcare business process and IT services team in the world”.

Our commitment


We strive to make a meaningful and sustainable impact on our customer’s business by providing market-leading services and products.

We will continually innovate by applying the insights from our cumulative knowledge to achieve the best results.

We will enable our employees and extended family, alumni, shareholders, and the communities in which we live to grow and thrive.

Our Values


Our values are the foundation of our culture and the people we aspire to be. We focus on delivering value to our customers by applying our expertise and skills and working with our partners.

HExcellence - Be focused on being great


Integrity - Be uncompromisingly honest and straightforward.


Innovation - Challenge the status quo to develop new-age solutions.


Respect - Treat everyone as we would like to be treated.


Courage - Never be afraid to do the right thing.


Compassion - Create a culture of warmth and belonging.


Fun - Enjoy every moment and have fun as we build a great company.
