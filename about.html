DNA - Access Healthcare

Our DNA

Revenue Cycle - Services. Technology. Automation

Since 2024, Orange Healthcare has been a trusted partner to healthcare institutions, providing Revenue Cycle solutions that enhance clinical outcomes, improve financial performance, and optimize operations. We leverage our revenue cycle expertise, advanced technology solutions, automation platforms, analytics, and best practices to deliver industry-leading results. In essence, we manage the business side of healthcare for our clients, allowing them to focus on patient care.

—— WHO WE ARE ——

A Reliable Partner Dedicated to Advancing
Healthcare Through Technology and Expertise


Healthcare institutions—including hospitals, healthcare systems, physician practices, and independent non-acute care providers—trust Orange Healthcare with their revenue cycle management. As a startup, we focus on reducing costs and accelerating cash flow through efficient billing, medical coding, payments, and collections processes. By applying innovative technology and best practices, we streamline clinical, administrative, and financial workflows, allowing our clients to focus on delivering high-quality patient care.


For health plans, Orange Healthcare offers a comprehensive suite of outsourced services, including member enrollment and engagement, claims administration, and provider management.

Our Heritage and History


Despite being a startup, Orange Healthcare is quickly adapting to the ever-changing landscape of the healthcare industry.


Our journey is defined by a strong focus on advanced technologies, innovation, and a commitment to delivering exceptional services that optimize revenue cycles and drive positive change for healthcare organizations.


Today, Orange Healthcare is positioning itself at the forefront of the industry, pushing boundaries and shaping the future of healthcare services through cutting-edge solutions and dedicated partnerships.


Our Purpose and Values


At Orange Healthcare, our purpose is to "Become the most respected healthcare business process and IT services team in the world." We are steadily advancing toward this goal, guided by a core set of values that define our journey. These values form the foundation of our culture and shape the people we aspire to be. By applying our expertise and skills, we focus on delivering exceptional value to our customers while collaborating with our partners to drive meaningful impact in the healthcare industry.


Our community service program


At Orange Healthcare, we are committed to enriching the communities where we live and work, alongside our stakeholders—team members, families, customers, and suppliers. Social responsibility is central to our business, and we integrate it into every aspect of our operations to make a positive, lasting impact.


School Adoption: Supporting underprivileged communities by financially assisting schools near our offices.


Hunger Elimination: Partnering with NTFB and Get Shift Done to provide meals for families during the pandemic.


Disaster Relief: Assisting communities in rebuilding after global calamities.


Swacch Bharat: Promoting cleanliness in our neighborhoods.


Blood Drives: Mobilizing donors to help offset the global shortage and save lives.


Tree Planting: Contributing to environmental sustainability by planting saplings on deforested land.
